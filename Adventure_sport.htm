Cage Rocket

The Cage Rocket is a handy storage unit for stashing keys, money, tools, gels, a mobile phone or sunglasses on a road or mountain bike.  The lightweight molded plastic container (3.7 ounces, 45 cubic inches) fits securely into water bottle cage and protects contents with a thin layer of foam padding and a secure closure latch.  No, a can of beer won't fit in it.
