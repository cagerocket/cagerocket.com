The Cage Rocket 

Storage device fills a growing need for road and mountain cyclists 

SEATTLE, WA USA - This Summer Cage Rocket, Inc. of Seattle, Washington is launching the first in a series of new bicycling products.  The Cage Rocket, made in the USA, is a lightweight, rugged storage container that slides snuggly into standard bicycle water bottle cages on bicycles.  It is intended for storing items such as tools, patch kits, food, phones, sunglasses, money, maps, first aid and more.  Its door can be opened, and the contents accessed without removing the Cage Rocket from the bike.   

  Key Features Of The Cage Rocket

·         Accessibility: It’s easy to pop open the Rocket door and access the stored articles with one hand.  

·         Portability: Transfer the Cage Rocket to any bike that has a cage.  Pull it out at the end of the ride.  No additional mounting brackets or straps required.

·         Durable Construction: Rugged High Density Polyethylene and ABS Materials.

·         Sleek Aero Design: Looks great on any bike.

·         Foam Lining: Protects your stored gear.  Eliminates chatter.

·         Lightweight: 105 grams!

  Why The Cage Rocket?

Because the Cage Rocket gives cyclists a convenient storage alternative for those items they want quick, easy access to, but don’t necessarily want to carry on their bodies.  Behind-the-seat bags require cyclists to completely dismount their bikes in order to access the contents.  The Cage Rocket provides storage space cyclists can access with one hand.  It’s foam lining protects your gear.  Plus, the Cage Rocket can be easily attached to any bike with a cage. Pretty neat, huh?
